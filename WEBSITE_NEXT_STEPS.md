# WEBSITE_NEXT_STEPS

## Goal (next 30 days)
Increase trust and conversion quality for logistics lead-gen buyers while keeping claims compliant and realistic.

## What was improved now
- Refined homepage positioning around **trust + qualified lead handoff**.
- Added clearer process framing (4-week pilot flow) and compliance language.
- Tightened quote form messaging to filter better-fit buyers.
- Upgraded services page with explicit QA criteria and reporting expectations.
- Synced updates in both site directories:
  - `preview/`
  - `smklog-site-v1/`

## High-impact integrations to connect next
1. **Form + CRM sync (must-have)**
   - Tool options: HubSpot Forms, Pipedrive Web Forms + Zapier/Make, Airtable + webhook.
   - Output fields required: lane, mode, monthly volume, timeline, job title, consent checkbox, source URL.
2. **Call booking + qualification gate**
   - Calendly (or TidyCal) after form submission with hidden fields from quote form.
3. **Email domain verification + warm sequence**
   - SPF/DKIM/DMARC configured, then intro + follow-up cadence for inbound form leads.
4. **Analytics + event tracking**
   - GA4 + Meta Pixel + LinkedIn Insight Tag (if ads planned).
   - Track: `quote_submit`, `cta_click`, `thank_you_view`, `book_call_click`.
5. **Session replay / UX diagnostics**
   - Microsoft Clarity or Hotjar to identify drop-offs in form steps.

## KPI targets for next 60 days
- Landing-to-form-start: **>= 3.5%**
- Form completion rate: **>= 28%**
- Qualified lead rate (from forms): **>= 45%**
- Show-up rate on booked calls: **>= 65%**

## Compliance guardrails
- No “guaranteed shipment/contracts” language.
- Store consent evidence per lead.
- Keep privacy/terms links visible near every form submit button.
- Maintain clear disqualification and refund/replacement policy in writing.
